Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I remove ads?

A: You can remove ads by purchasing the "Remove Ads" option from the Settings menu within the app.

Q: How do I reset my progress?

A: Go to Settings > Reset Progress to clear all your learning data and start fresh.

Q: Is my data backed up?

A: Your learning progress is stored locally on your device. Make sure to keep your device backed up.

Q: What languages are supported?

A: Onoma Step supports English, Korean, and Chinese interfaces.

Q: How many words are included?

A: The app includes over 1,200 Japanese onomatopoeia and mimetic words organized into 40+ categories.
